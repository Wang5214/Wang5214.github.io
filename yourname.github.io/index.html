Wei Wang

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Bristol, UK, supervised by Prof. Angela Doufexi and Prof. Mark A. Beach, with a China Scholarship Council-University of Bristol PhD Scholarship. I also completed an M.Sc. (with Distinction) in Wireless Communications and Signal Processing at the University of Bristol in 2021. Prior to that, I earned a B.Eng. in Radio Wave Propagation and Antennas from Tianjin University of Technology, China in 2020.

My Ph.D. programme is on Reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS), with the main focus on discrete phase shift optimization through super heuristic algorithms and deep reinforcement learning (DRL) algorithms. Meanwhile, I am also intersted in the hardware design of RIS and simultaneously transmitting and reflecting (STAR) RIS.
